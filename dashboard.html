Dog Vaccinations

Core Vaccines:

  • Rabies
  • DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza)

Non-Core Vaccines:

  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough)
  • Leptospirosis
  • Lyme Disease

Vaccination Schedule:

Age Vaccination
6-8 weeks DHPP
12 weeks Rabies
1 year Booster shots for DHPP and Rabies

Cat Vaccinations

Core Vaccines:

  • Rabies
  • FVRCP (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, and Panleukopenia)

Non-Core Vaccines:

  • Feline Leukemia Virus (FeLV)
  • Chlamydophila felis

Vaccination Schedule:

Age Vaccination
6-8 weeks FVRCP
12 weeks Rabies
1 year Booster shots for FVRCP and Rabies
